Tool Room Facility

Precision machining and tooling supported by high-performance machines in a controlled environment.

Area:

The tool room occupies about 20,000 sq.ft., organized into CNC machining centers, wire cutting EDM, conventional machines, tool assembly zones, and tool storage racks. This controlled environment supports fine machining, tool designing, and maintenance tasks. The area layout ensures low vibration, high stability, and dust control to maintain tool precision and cleanliness.

Vertical Machining Centres

Vertical Machining Centres play a vital role in the precision manufacturing of automotive components. These advanced machines are designed for high-speed, accurate operations, allowing us to produce complex parts with tight tolerances and exceptional consistency. By utilizing Vertical Machining Centres, we ensure efficient production processes, reduced lead times, and high-quality output that meets the rigorous standards of the automotive industry. Their reliability and performance contribute significantly to maintaining the excellence of our products.

Wire Cutting EDM

Tool accuracy is vital, and our quality team uses profile projectors, tool presetters, gauge blocks, coordinate measuring machines (CMM), and surface roughness testers to verify every tool's precision. Tool trials are documented, and adjustments are made until zero deviation is achieved. Regular maintenance logs and inspection certificates ensure long-term reliability.
