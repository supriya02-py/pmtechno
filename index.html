Get to know us

Founded in 1992 in Bengaluru, our company began with a clear vision and a commitment to engineering excellence. From modest beginnings, we have grown steadily to become a trusted name in the automotive component and sheet metal fabrication industry, serving both OEMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers) and Tier 1 suppliers. With over three decades of industry experience, we pride ourselves on delivering technologically advanced solutions that meet the evolving demands of our esteemed customers. Our success has been driven by our core values of quality, innovation, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We specialize in the design, development, and precision manufacturing of automotive components and sheet metal assemblies. Leveraging cutting-edge machinery and state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure our products meet the highest standards of durability, dimensional accuracy, and performance.
